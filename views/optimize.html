Optimized bus routes




Bus routes need to be updated according to their usage by the people.

Bus routes in a lot of cities are not updated for decades. New bus routes can be created using the following:

  • Existing bus routes
  • Population chart of city - Heat Map
  • Average Ticket sales of each bus stand

An algorithm to provide detours in bus routes or modifications to the existing routes, including or excluding bus stands with highest population density as the primary criterion.

Optimization has many advantages -

Improves the traffic scenario across the whole city.
Mitigation of pernicious pollution caused by buses in the populated regions of the city.
